Our Curriculum

Explore the diverse academic programs designed to shape global learners.

CBSE

Pavna School follows the CBSE curriculum, delivering a structured academic journey from Pre-Primary to Senior Secondary. Early years emphasise activity-based learning, phonics, and the LSRW framework to build strong foundational skills. Higher classes adopt project-based, concept-centric teaching to foster analytical thinking and cross-curricular understanding. All academic and evaluation practices strictly adhere to the latest CBSE guidelines.

Montessori

Pavna School is known for delivering quality education to children from the pre-primary school level up to the senior secondary level. It is, however, the Montessori wing that is truly specialized in early education. With its scientifically designed materials, a more modern approach to pedagogy, and an environment filled with love, nurturing every possibility for growth, it can fit every wish a parent might have for their child's formative years.

Cambridge

Pavna School offers the Cambridge curriculum across four progressive stages — Primary, Lower Secondary, IGCSE, and A Levels — providing a globally recognised and rigorous academic pathway. Each stage builds seamlessly on the previous, nurturing enquiry-based learning, critical thinking, and conceptual understanding from an early age. IGCSE equips students with a broad subject foundation, while A Levels offer specialised depth to prepare them for top universities worldwide.

Frequently Asked Questions

What curriculums does Pavna School offer?
Pavna School offers dual academic pathways to cater to diverse learning needs. Students can choose between the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) for a robust national curriculum or the Cambridge Assessment International Education (CAIE) for a global perspective.
Is boarding mandatory for all students?
While we pride ourselves on our world-class residential facilities, boarding is not mandatory for all grades. We offer Day Boarding (8 AM - 5 PM), Weekly Boarding (Mon-Fri), and Full Boarding options to provide flexibility for families.
What is the student-teacher ratio?
To ensure personalized attention and mentorship, we maintain a strict student-teacher ratio of 1:12 across all grades. In our Early Years program, this ratio is even lower at 1:8 to support developmental needs.
How does the admission process work?
The process begins with an online enquiry or campus tour booking. Following this, there is an interaction/assessment session depending on the grade applied for. Once selected, parents can complete the documentation and fee payment to secure the seat.
